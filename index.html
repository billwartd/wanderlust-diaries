About

Hi, I'm Alex — Traveler, Writer, Dreamer

I quit my corporate job in 2019 to explore the world full-time. What started as a personal journal became Wanderlust Diaries — a place where travel stories come alive. I believe that the best adventures happen when you step outside your comfort zone and embrace the unknown.

Over 5 years, I've visited 47 countries across 6 continents. Every destination taught me something new about the world and myself. This blog is my way of sharing those lessons — and inspiring you to take that trip you've been dreaming about.
